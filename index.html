Rules of Play:
We don't just play the game however we want. There are certain rules we must follow for these records to be considered valid and fair. Anyone who has played TSB enough knows there are ways to cheat the system, so here are our rules:

  • No lurching, which is where you dive at the QB immediately after the snap. Defensive lineman used must grapple or drop back before pursuing the QB. All other defenders have no limits.
  • No onside kicks unless tied or trailing in the 4th quarter or overtime. Any accidental onside kicks must be punted away.
  • When defending kicking attempts, use of 4th player down (rolb) is prohibited due to ability to run straight by the blockers.

We used to not allow WRs to be used in the backfield on traditional running players, but have since changed that rule to allow freedom of position swapping. After all, no WR is going to be Bo Jackson or Christian Okoye.

As this is all enforced via honor system. there are a few other considerations: We look down upon using save states to constantly re-try to play games for records. In general, we just play the games and they fall as they may.
